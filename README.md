Toyota-Stock-Analysis

Toyota Stock Analysis 1980–2025
Tools: MySQL · Power BI  
Data: 11,542 daily trading records across 45 years

Business Questions
- How has Toyota's stock price grown over 45 years?
- Which decade performed best?
- How did the 2008 financial crisis impact the stock?
- Which years were most volatile?

Key Findings
- Toyota stock grew from $3.24 in 1980 to $254.77 in 2025 — over 7,800% growth
- 2024 was the best year ever with an average price of $201
- The 2008 financial crisis caused a 37% drop — the worst in 45 years
- 2022 and 2008 were the most volatile years with average daily swings of $2.46
- 2025 saw the biggest single day price swing ever at $14.48
